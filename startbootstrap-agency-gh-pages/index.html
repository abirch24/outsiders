Our fight

No one is going to give us a say unless we stand up and stand together. So help us gather our voice and make a stand, and as one say “NO!”

Pot a Plant

Our gift to you

In order to reward you for your efforts and dedication to our cause, the Outsider would like to gift you a plant. With the seeds you gain during the Espionage Mission, you will be able to take home your own plant as a memorial of your service.

  • Location: UWB Food Garden

Be Our Spy

Infiltrate Midnight Industries

The outsiders have a mission for new recruits. Infiltrate the offices of Midnight Industries, follow your instructions to locate and scan documents about their plans to build over the wetlands against the wishes of everyone who lives here.

  • Location: Motherbase

Raise Your Voice

Create a poster to share our message.

The numbers of the outsiders are growing, and we need your help to get all our new members a way to show their support and energy. Take a poster and write a something spreading our message and mission or find advertisements from midnight industries and put our messages over theirs to show them we mean business.

  • Location: UWB Plaza
