Our fight

Battling corporate greed

Midnight Industries has recently begun a marketing campaign to garner support for their plans to bulldoze the wetlands in favor of commercial development of the area. This development has been spearheaded by a corporate conglomerate seeking to expand the opportunities for capitalist gains in the local area. Midnight Industries has begun maneuvering paid agents to aggressively lobby for reformation of land rights and the end of the protected status of the wetlands. This act is viewed by the Outsiders as an attack on our environmental freedoms and our attempts at culling the rate of climate impact due to heavy development.
As Outsiders we understand that this quest for expansion is a money grab tactic. Midnight Industries has put out propaganda to influence the minds of the people. Midnight Industries claims that the development is good for economic growth, the newly developed buildings will be high priced luxury apartments, with an “natural and beautiful views” selling point which only the wealthy can afford. The shopping centers located within the development will also bring higher business rents, pushing out local and small businesses in favor of large chain stores or niche stores where only affluent people would shop.


We need your help! As a member of the outsider faction it falls on you and other likeminded people to help us in our fight against this monolithic corporate beast. No one is going to give us a say unless we stand up and stand together. So help us gather our voice and make a stand, and as one say “No!”
