Motherbase

The Control Center

Here, we would officially like to welcome you to the Outsiders! After you've created posters for our cause the Motherbase will be your official recruitment stage. Now we know you've got what it takes to be an Outsider. It is here you'll be debriefed on the Outsiders mission statement, and gear up for your Espionage Mission. NOTE: You MUST visit the Motherbase before starting your Espionage Mission! We have the necessary equipment you'll need in order to ellude Midnight Industries and succeed in the rest of your journey.

  • Location: UWB Food Garden / Cascadia Trailer

Pot a Plant

Our gift to you

To reward you for your efforts and dedication to our cause, the Outsiders would like to gift you a plant. With the seeds you gain during the Espionage Mission, you will be able to take home your own plant as a memorial of your service.

  • Location: UWB Food Garden

Be Our Spy

Infiltrate Midnight Industries

After receiving your gear and supplies from the Mother Base, you will be our spy to infiltrate the offices of Midnight Industries. Your mission, should you choose to accept it, will be to locate and scan documents about Midnight Industries's plans to build over the wetlands. Midnight Industries knows our faces, so that's why we need YOU. The fate of the wetlands rests in your hands.

  • Location: Motherbase

Raise Your Voice

Create a poster to share our message.

The first stop of your Outsider recruitment. The numbers of the Outsiders are growing, and we need your help to get all our new members a way to show their support and energy. Take a poster and write something to spread our message and mission. The first step in raising our voices starts here! Join the fight, raise your voice, and start your Outsider journey.

  • Location: UWB Plaza
